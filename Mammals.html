African Elephant

The largest land animal, with massive ears and a trunk. Herbivorous and social.

Bengal Tiger

A powerful carnivore with striking orange and black stripes. Found in Indian forests.

Bottlenose Dolphin

Intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and echolocation skills.

Lion

Known as the "King of the Jungle," lions are social big cats living in prides across Africa.

Monkey

Intelligent and social primates found in forests around the world, often agile and playful.

Panda

Giant pandas are gentle mammals known for their black-and-white fur and love of bamboo. They are native to China and are a symbol of wildlife conservation.
